How this hospital is priced — the payer multiplier graph

Every plan's negotiated rate as a multiple of the free public CMS schedule (OPPS for procedures/imaging, CLFS for labs), for one service line. Higher bar = more expensive deal. Real per-code medians, n-gated (>=12 codes).
One hospital, one service line at a time (a plan's multiplier varies by line). Bars are the median × CMS across that plan's codes. %-of-charges hospitals are flagged — there the "× CMS" is a gross artifact, not a negotiated multiple. Data: byte-exact AZ MRF captures + CMS OPPS Addendum B / CLFS 2025.
